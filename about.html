Welcome to TOM YUM KUNG, where the vibrant flavors of Thailand come to life! Located in the heart of Fishers, our family- owned restaurant offers an authentic Thai dining experience, blending traditional recipes with fresh, local ingredients. From our fragrant curries and sizzling stir-fries to our deliciously tangy papaya salads, each dish is crafted with passion and care. Whether you're joining us for a casual lunch, a special dinner, or a quick takeaway, we invite you to savor the rich, aromatic tastes of Thai cuisine in a warm and welcoming atmosphere. Come experience the true essence of Thailand with us!
